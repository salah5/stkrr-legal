# Privacy Policy for Stkrr

**Last Updated:** November 2024

## 1. Introduction

Stkrr ("App", "we", "us", or "our") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and otherwise handle your information when you use our mobile application available on iOS.

Please read this Privacy Policy carefully. If you do not agree with our policies and practices, please do not use our App.

## 2. Information We Collect

### 2.1 Photos and Images
- **What:** Photos and images you select within the App
- **How:** You explicitly choose which photos to upload
- **Purpose:** To process and transform photos into sticker art using AI
- **Storage:** Photos are processed in real-time and not stored by us. Only the generated stickers are saved to your device.

### 2.2 Generated Stickers
- **What:** AI-generated sticker artwork created from your photos
- **Storage:** Generated stickers are stored locally on your device only
- **Backup:** iOS may backup your sticker data if you use iCloud backup
- **Purpose:** To allow you to save, organize, and share your generated stickers

### 2.3 Usage Data
- **What:**
  - Number of stickers generated daily (for free tier limit enforcement)
  - Premium subscription status
  - Style preferences
- **Storage:** Stored locally on your device using standard iOS storage mechanisms
- **Purpose:** To enforce free tier daily limits and personalize your experience

### 2.4 Purchase Information
- **What:** Subscription purchases and in-app payment data
- **Handled By:** Apple App Store
- **Our Role:** We receive confirmation that a purchase occurred, but do not collect payment details (Apple handles all payment processing)
- **Storage:** Not stored by us; managed by Apple
- **Purpose:** To grant premium features to paying subscribers

### 2.5 Device Information
- **What:** We do not collect device identifiers, advertising IDs, or device names
- **What We Do Collect:** Basic device model and OS version (through standard analytics if enabled)
- **Purpose:** To ensure app compatibility and performance

## 3. How We Use Your Information

### 3.1 Photo Processing
Your selected photos are sent to **OpenAI's API** for AI processing to generate sticker artwork:
- Photos are processed in real-time
- We do not retain copies of your original photos
- OpenAI processes your photos according to their privacy policy and usage policies
- OpenAI may use processed images for service improvement (see OpenAI's privacy policy)

### 3.2 Service Improvement
- Usage patterns help us understand which sticker styles are popular
- We use this information to improve the App and add new features
- All usage data is processed locally on your device

### 3.3 Free Tier Management
- Your daily sticker count is used to enforce the free tier daily limit (3 stickers per day)
- This prevents abuse of the free service

### 3.4 Premium Features
- Your subscription status is stored to unlock premium sticker styles
- We do not use your information for marketing or advertising

## 4. Third-Party Services

### 4.1 OpenAI API
- **Service:** AI-powered image processing
- **Data Shared:** Your selected photos (only when you request sticker generation)
- **Privacy:** Subject to OpenAI's Privacy Policy (https://openai.com/privacy)
- **Data Handling:** OpenAI may use your data according to their usage policies

### 4.2 Apple App Store
- **Service:** Payment processing and app distribution
- **Data Handled:** Payment information, subscription status
- **Privacy:** Subject to Apple's Privacy Policy (https://www.apple.com/privacy)

### 4.3 WhatsApp (Optional)
- **Service:** Sharing stickers to WhatsApp (optional feature)
- **Data Shared:** Only the generated sticker image you choose to share
- **Privacy:** Subject to WhatsApp's Privacy Policy

## 5. Data Retention

### 5.1 How Long We Keep Your Data
- **Photos:** Not stored; processed and discarded immediately
- **Generated Stickers:** Kept on your device until you delete them
- **Usage Data:** Kept on your device until app data is cleared or app is uninstalled
- **Purchase Data:** Retained by Apple for subscription management (we only receive confirmation)

### 5.2 Backup Data
- If you enable iCloud Backup, iOS may backup your sticker data
- This is subject to Apple's iCloud privacy terms
- You can disable iCloud backup for app data in your device settings

## 6. Data Security

We implement appropriate technical measures to protect your data:

- **Local Storage:** All personal data is encrypted using iOS's standard encryption
- **API Communication:** HTTPS encryption for all API communications
- **API Keys:** Securely managed and never exposed in source code
- **No Cloud Storage:** We do not store your photos or stickers on our servers

However, no method of transmission over the Internet or electronic storage is 100% secure. We cannot guarantee absolute security.

## 7. Your Privacy Rights

### 7.1 Data Access & Deletion
- All your data is stored locally on your device
- You can delete any sticker by removing it from the app
- You can delete all app data by uninstalling the app
- On iOS, you can also clear app data in Settings > General > iPhone Storage

### 7.2 Tracking & Advertising
- We do not track you across other apps or websites
- We do not use your data for targeted advertising
- We do not sell your data to third parties

### 7.3 Age Restrictions
This App is intended for users 13 years and older. We do not knowingly collect information from children under 13.
